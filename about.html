About Us

Horse Creek AI

Rooted in Appalachia. Powered by AI.

Our Mission

Horse Creek AI exists to help small businesses, educators, nonprofits, and rural entrepreneurs take advantage of powerful AI tools—without the jargon, high costs, or technical barriers. We believe innovation belongs to everyone.

Who We Serve

Our clients include Appalachian startups, school districts, healthcare clinics, churches, local governments, and small-town businesses. We’re based in West Virginia and understand the unique challenges and opportunities of working in rural spaces.

Meet the Owner

Dr. Vincent Smith

Dr. Vincent Smith is the founder of Horse Creek AI and serves as the Program Director of Data Analytics and Computer Science at the University of Charleston. With a PhD in Data Science and multiple master’s degrees in Mathematics and Psychology, Dr. Smith brings a rare blend of technical expertise and practical teaching experience.

Born and based in rural West Virginia, he has spent his career teaching, mentoring, and helping underserved communities access tools that make a real difference. Through Horse Creek AI, he’s focused on delivering affordable, jargon-free AI solutions to local nonprofits, small businesses, and educators across Appalachia.

Expertise: Applied data science, AI integration, curriculum design, statistical modeling, and ethical use of technology.

Fun Fact: Vincent is also a game developer and vacation rental business owner, combining creativity with data in everything he does.
